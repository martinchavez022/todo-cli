

## Why and how to track your habits

**resource: ** https://jamesclear.com/habit-tracker

#### Here's why:

If you cannot measure you can performe, that's why elite performers will often measure, quantify, and tracj their progress in various ways.

> Each little measurement provides feedback

#### Benefits of tracking is powerful

1. A habit tracker reminds  you to act.
2. A habit tracker motivates you to continue.
3. A habit tracker provides immediate satisfaction.

#### Recommendations.

One of the most powerful tips to keep do a habit trought the time is have habits in two-minute rule, which suggests you scale your habits down until they take two minutes or less to perform.
